About

Good interfaces don’t just look clean — they feel deliberate. That idea shapes the way I approach frontend development, where I focus on building responsive and visually balanced web experiences that work smoothly across devices.

Much of my work revolves around designing and developing concept brand websites and modern landing webpages. I focus on layout, typography, and interaction, making sure every element has a purpose and the interface feels consistent rather than overdone.

Outside of work, I enjoy watching movies and anime, and I regularly play chess. Both influence how I think — from visual storytelling and pacing to planning, structure, and decision-making — and these ways of thinking often reflect in my design and problem-solving process.

I’m currently open to freelance work and frontend internships, and I’m keen on contributing to real-world projects where I can add value while continuing to improve my craft.
